## Adding a new site

Not on the list? Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/bohmaan/AxgstAIO/issues) describing the site's checkout flow. Sites that use standard Shopify, Magento, or public REST APIs are quick to add. Sites with heavy Akamai/DataDome protection require more reverse-engineering effort and may not be possible.
